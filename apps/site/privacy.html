Privacy Policy

Effective June 27, 2026

Coach AI respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use our text-based fitness coaching service.

Information we collect

We may collect your mobile phone number, messages you send to Coach AI, workout logs, preferences, and injury notes that you voluntarily share, along with basic service records needed to deliver the service.

How we use information

We use this information to send workout reminders, record workout activity, provide coaching replies, respond to support requests, and maintain the security and operation of Coach AI. Data is used only to provide and operate the coaching service.

Information sharing

Mobile phone numbers are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may use service providers only as necessary to operate Coach AI, deliver messages, comply with law, or protect the service and its users.

Text messaging notice: Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. We use reasonable safeguards to protect the information we maintain.

Your choices

You may stop text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for assistance.

Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
